Alfredo

Description

This rich and creamy fettuccine Alfredo recipe is easy enough for beginner cooks, but it’s impressive enough for company. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, but let's go over the basics!

Ingredients

  • 24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup butter
  • ¾ pint heavy cream
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 dash garlic salt
  • ¾ cup grated Romano cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Step-By-Step

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
